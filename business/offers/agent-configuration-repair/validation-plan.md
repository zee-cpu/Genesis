# First revenue validation plan

Status: internal draft; external communication and payment collection are not authorized by this file.

## Decision

Decide whether developers with broken AI-agent JSON configurations will pay $49 for a bounded repair and validation package.

## Target customer

Independent developers and small AI software teams that currently have a specific failing prompt/tool configuration and lack time or confidence to repair it themselves.

## Hypothesis

At least one qualified prospect out of 20 will purchase the defined $49 repair package after reviewing its scope and example.

## Baseline

Zero verified offers, zero qualified prospect responses, and zero paid repairs for this service. The previous asset-engine experiment is technical evidence only; it is not customer-demand evidence.

## Proposed evidence sequence

1. Obtain Human Authority approval for the exact outreach message, audience, channel, privacy boundary, and operator.
2. Contact no more than 20 qualified prospects through the approved channel.
3. Record response counts without collecting unnecessary personal data.
4. Preserve objections and explicit counterevidence.
5. Do not claim validation unless a real payment is completed and the service is delivered.

## Metrics

- Primary: `paid_customers`, calculated as a count with pass threshold `>= 1`.
- Secondary: `qualified_positive_responses / qualified_prospects_contacted`.
- Guardrails: complaints, unwanted follow-ups, sensitive-data submissions, refunds, actual labor hours, and delivery failures.

## Proposed limits

- Cash: $0 before separately approved payment infrastructure.
- Outreach volume: maximum 20 qualified prospects.
- Follow-ups: maximum one per non-responsive prospect, only if approved.
- Data: public business contact information and minimal internal response notes only.
- Stop immediately on a complaint, sensitive-data submission, platform-policy concern, or material scope mismatch.

## Pass, revise, and stop logic

- Pass: at least one completed $49 payment and delivery satisfying the written acceptance checks.
- Revise: credible problem confirmation but no purchase; review price, trust, urgency, and channel evidence before changing the offer.
- Stop/archive: no credible problem confirmation, material complaints, unsafe data requirements, or delivery economics that exceed the bounded price.

## Missing evidence before Genesis initialization

Do not fabricate the `start-business` evidence fields. The first valid record needs a real source reference, factual summary, provenance, and counterevidence from an observed customer or an attributable public source.
